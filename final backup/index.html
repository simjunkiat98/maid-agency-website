About Us

At JLK Employment Services, we understand that hiring a domestic helper is more than just filling a role-it’s about welcoming a trusted member into your home. Based in the heart of Singapore, we specialize in connecting families with experienced, reliable, and well-trained foreign domestic workers who perfectly match your unique household needs.

With years of expertise navigating Singapore’s regulatory landscape and a deep commitment to personalized service, we guide you every step of the way-from selecting the right helper to ensuring a smooth transition and ongoing support. Our dedicated consultants take the time to understand your expectations and lifestyle, so we can recommend candidates who not only meet your requirements but also bring harmony and peace of mind to your home.

We pride ourselves on transparency, professionalism, and care. Our helpers undergo comprehensive training to equip them with the skills and confidence to assist with household duties, childcare, or eldercare. Beyond placement, we offer continuous performance reviews and responsive support, ensuring your satisfaction and your helper’s well-being.

Let us help you find the perfect domestic helper to enhance your family’s quality of life-because your home deserves nothing less.

FAQs

Top 5 Frequently Asked Questions!

The main costs include:

  • Agency fee (typically 2-3 months' salary)
  • Maid's monthly salary (minimum S$650 as of 2024)
  • Employment agency fee (S$150)
  • Foreign worker levy (S$60 per month)
  • Insurance (mandatory)
  • Medical examination fees

Once you have selected your helper, we will begin the processing for her. If her documents are ready (eg. passport), her work permit application should take less than a week for approval.

Once her work permit is approved, and if she requires no other processing, it will take about a month for us to arrange for your maid to arrive. Your maid’s arrival date also largely depends on the availability of the Entry Approval date issued by MOM.

Once her work permit is approved, and if she requires other forms of document processing (depending on her country’s requirements and legislation) it will take about 2 months for your maid to arrive. Her arrival date also largely depends on the availability of the Entry Approval date issued by MOM.

After which, she has to proceed for her medical check-up and may have to complete the Settling In Programme (course required for all foreign domestic worker working in Singapore for the first time) before she can start work.

In addition, she will be required to pass her medical examination and issue her work permit within 14 days of arriving in Singapore.

Transfer maids are migrant domestic workers (MDWs) who are already in Singapore under the employment other employers and are seeking a new employer.

This usually occurs when the 2 years contract of the maid is expiring or anytime when an employer or helper chooses to terminate or discontinue the employment service for whatsoever reasons.

You may have a space constraint in a 2-room HDB flat with only 1 bedroom. We will consider your eligibility and need to employ a foreign domestic worker (FDW). You will also have to ensure sufficient privacy and sleeping space in the house where the FDW will be staying.

As per MOM requirements, Employers will need to pay their helper's salary on time, bear all the living expenses of the helper and be responsible for her welfare while she is in Singapore.
